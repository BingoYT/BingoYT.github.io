About me


My name is Alex. I'm 16 years of age and currently living in Kharkiv, Ukraine. I am one of those who like maths and coding. But what I really love doing is setting up new challenges and completing them before the specific term. I'm an aspired computer science student whose goal is to become a data scientist. Here is the list of my favourite subjects in school:

Maths

Maths is by far my favourite school subject. There are some things I utterly hate in it. They are: proofs of theorems and theoretical lectures. However, what makes me on cloud nine is the feeling of solving the problem I couldn't figure out for ages.

Computing

Computing (in our country it's called Informatics) is also one of my favourite school subjects. It was quite a surprise for me that I like it as I always considered it as some boring coding thing. Actually, that's what I'm doing right now! :)

Foreign Languages

At the age of about 8 I realised my talent when it comes to foreign languages. It sounds implausible, but two years ago I couldn't speak English at all. Now, as I started to attend extra classes, my English became more fluent and in some way advanced.
